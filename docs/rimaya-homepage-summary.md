# Rimaya — Homepage Structure at a Glance

**Payroll · Recruitment · Consulting** — A lead-generation website for two audiences: **businesses** (who need payroll, recruitment, or consulting) and **candidates** (who are looking for jobs). Every section is designed to build trust and move the visitor toward one of two actions: **Get a Quote** or **Apply Now**.

**Brand colours:** `#005597` (deep blue — structure & trust) · `#006dc1` (bright blue — buttons & actions) · white. **Sharp corners throughout.**

---

| Section | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| **1. Header / Navigation** | Keep the business door open at all times. A centred logo with clean links, and one bold **"Get a Quote"** button that stays visible on every page as the visitor scrolls. |
| **2. Hero** | Instantly show what Rimaya does and who it's for — leading with **Payroll** (the flagship). Two clear buttons let each visitor pick their path: **"Get a Payroll Quote"** for businesses and **"View Jobs"** for candidates. |
| **3. Trust Strip** | Give a stranger a reason to trust within the first 5 seconds — customer rating (★ 4.4), Established 2022, VAT registered, and the **A-rated sponsor licence**. |
| **4. Three Service Pillars** | Route the visitor to their need. Three cards — **Payroll Solutions, Recruitment, Consulting** — each with a one-line promise and its own button to that service page. Payroll gets subtle priority as the flagship. |
| **5. Why Rimaya** | Answer the "why you and not a competitor?" question with real reasons: Cost Efficiency, Quickest Turnaround, Risk Mitigated, Extensive Network, and Flexible Terms. |
| **6. Candidate Band** | Feed the candidate funnel. A section aimed at job seekers showing 2–3 live vacancies with a **"Browse all jobs / Apply now"** button. Every CV uploaded grows the talent database. |
| **7. Testimonials** | Build confidence with real customer reviews, shown cleanly. Proof that others trusted Rimaya and it worked. |
| **8. Final Call to Action** | Catch everyone who read the whole page without clicking. One dominant **"Get a Quote / Request a Callback"** action, with the reassurance line *"We reply within 12 hours."* |
| **9. Footer** | Provide utility and a final trust anchor — real UK office address, phone, email, quick links, socials, and a WhatsApp contact button. |

---

## The full website (all pages)

| Page | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| **Home** | The conversion journey above — help each visitor self-select, build trust, and reach a decision. |
| **Payroll Solutions** *(flagship)* | The deepest, most persuasive page. Full B2B payroll — processing, compliance, reporting — plus adjacent bookkeeping/accounts capability and the systems used (Xero, Sage, QuickBooks). Ends with **"Get a Payroll Quote."** |
| **Recruitment** | Temporary and permanent recruitment pitch backed by real stats, leading straight into the live job board. Ends with **"Hire with us"** (employers) and **"Browse jobs"** (candidates). |
| **Consulting** | A distinct service page following the same rhythm — intro, what's included, why Rimaya, and **"Book a consultation."** *(Content to be provided.)* |
| **Jobs** | A live, filterable job board (keyword, location, type, remote). Each job opens to a full description and an **Apply form with CV upload** — emailed to the recruiter with the CV attached. |
| **About** | The company story and credentials front and centre (Established 2022, VAT registered, A-rated sponsor licence, registration number) plus company values. Ends with **"Get in touch."** |
| **Contact** | The general lead-catcher — an enquiry form that reliably delivers to the inbox, full office details, the 12-hour response promise, and WhatsApp quick-contact. |
